Who are we?

The Nigerian Electricity Supply Corporation (Nigeria) Limited commenced operations in Nigeria in 1929 and has developed and operates generation, transmission and distribution systems to supply areas within the Plateau State and adjacent states. It is an investor-owned electric utility company with a generation capacity of 175GWhrs per annum from both hydro-electric and thermal sources of power. It serves an area in excess of 14,000 square kilometres across the Plateau State, Benue State, Nasarawa State, Kaduna State, the Federal Capital Territory and adjacent states and supplies mining, industrial consumers and domestic consumers in these areas.
